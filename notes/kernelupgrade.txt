Kernel upgrade notes for zero-unity.
* mkinitcpio for profile "linux" currently builds the following at /boot:
  - initramfs-linux-fallback.img
  - initramfs-linux.img
  - vmlinuz-linux
* TODO: Auto-back up kernel before upgrade, or manually:
  1. cp -vr /lib/modules/3.13.8-1-ARCH/ /lib/modules/3.13.8-1-ARCH-stable
  2. pacman -Syu  # will remove old packages, including kernel.
  3. mv -v /lib/modules/3.13.8-1-ARCH-stable /lib/modules/3.13.8-1-ARCH
  4. At this point the "stable" and "rescue" boot entries will still
    work, even if their initial ramdisk images are for the previous
    kernel versoin
